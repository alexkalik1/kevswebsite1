About YSK

I'm just a young artist on the path to enlightenment. I like too view the world as a beautiful and chaotic place, everything is crazy, and I am too. I want to capture what's in my head and slather my art over everyone through all mediums.

Inspiration

My inspirations incude: Johnny Gloom (tattoo artist), Bassnectar (music producer), Banksy (street artist), my brother (some dude), BROCKHAMPTON (independent artist collective). My biggest inspiration is the artists from Brazil known as "Bicicleta Sem Freio" because they know the value of simply wanting to create and do their shit independently.
